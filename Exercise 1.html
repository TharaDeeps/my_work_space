Let's Started...Pondicherry (or Puducherry), a French colonial settlement in India until 1954,
is now a Union Territory town bounded by the southeastern Tamil Nadu state.
Its French legacy is preserved in its French Quarter, with tree-lined streets, mustard-colored
colonial villas and chic boutiques. A seaside promenade runs along the Bay of Bengal and passes several statues,
including a 4m-high Gandhi Memorial.
